About me

I am a software developer with C-Level management experience who feels comfortable interfacing between software engineers, business stakeholders and product owners.

I design and implement CI/CD automation because I believe that it improves engineer's velocity and reduces regulatory overhead, thus providing a huge benefit to the business.

I dedicate my career to improving how to create highly productive development teams. My aim is to release quality software while achieving the deadlines negotiated with the stakeholders.

As a CTO, I am convinced that even with the management overhead, it is still key to contribute with code in some way, as I cannot envision leading a development team without being a developer myself.

As a DevOps engineer, I love to stay up to date with cloud native approaches to give a solution even in the most complex and demanding environments like fintech and health tech.

I have performed due diligence of software development teams and technology startups to assess the degree of quality and performance.

In a nutshell, I leverage my lifelong experience in many different software development teams to prevent technical problems and risks before they happen.
